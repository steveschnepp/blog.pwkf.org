--- layout: post title: "Easy Distributed SVN on a USB Stick With SVK" date: 2010-01-12 13:58:55 +0100 tags: [] ---
  1. setup SVK as usual with FSFS (BDB might work, but why bother ?)
  2. move $HOME/.svk/local (the svn repo) to your USB stick
  3. use of depotmap, describes by the author and by a tutorial
  4. edit $HOME/.svk/config to point "depotmap" to the moved local svn repos
  5. Repeat the same process (but not copying the repo to the stick) on each of you computer.
  6. Enjoy distributed work.
